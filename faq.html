Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tiaser Square?

In 2019, Tiaser Square restaurant was stablished in Canada by two brazilians and one colombian guys. With the intention to show the best of South America cuisine.

What type of food do we have?

At Tiaser Square we offer the most famous plates from Brazil and Colombia.

Are reservations required?

Reservations are not required; however, they are encouraged, especially for the holidays and on weekends. Reservations may be made online at, tiasersquare.com/reservation/. You may also contact your local Tiaser Square via phone.

Do you any Birthday specials by any chance?

It would be our privilege to share in your birthday celebration. Our unique concept is exciting and interactive for all special events. We do offer a complimentary dessert for the guest of honor. Make sure to sign up for the Tiaser eClub for the latest news, events and promotions.

Do you have children's menu?

The Tiaser Square offers a special menu for kids.

What credit cards are accepted?

American Express, Diners Club, Discover, JCB, MasterCard and Visa

Do you offer Gluten Free options?

We have many wonderful gluten free options. Please contact your local Tiaser Square and ask to speak with the manager or email us at info@tiasersquare.com.

Do you have All You Can Eat options?

No. We still don't have all you can eat options, but we are working on it.

Where does the beef in Canada comes from?

We serve 100% Canadian beef, aged a minimum of 21 days to enhance the flavor and tenderness of each cut.

Do you ever share your recipes?

Please contact your local Tiaser Square and ask to speak with the manager or email us at info@tiasersquare.com for questions on select recipes.

Do you offer franchising opportunities?

Yes, Tiaser Square is considering international franchising opportunities. Please contact Tiago Soriano tiagosoriano@fogo.com with questions regarding business expansion outside Canada.

Who are your suppliers?

We have enjoyed long tenures with the most widely respected and responsible suppliers in the business. Our partners share our “Respeito Pela Comida” (“respect for food”), and we continually work together to ensure ongoing mutual guidance and transparency so we can deliver safe, high quality and delicious food to our guests.
