Colombian and Brazilian Food

Bandeja Paisa

Red beans, fried pork rind, rice, meat, fried egg, platain, pork sausage, arepa and avocado.

Mute Santandereano

Soup with beef, beef ribs, pork meat, tripe, pasta, hominy corn, potatoes, pumpkin, chickpeas, canary beans, and carrots.

Moqueca

Halibut, swordfish or cor, lime, olive oil, onions, paprika, red pepper, cilantro, garlic and coconut milk.

Feijoada

Black beans, onions, ham, ham hocks, bacon, bay leaves, cilantro, fresh parsley.
